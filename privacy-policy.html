3. Cookies

Cookies are files with a small amount of data commonly used as unique anonymous identifiers. These are sent to your browser from websites you visit and are stored on your device's internal memory.

We use cookies to track website activity, measure marketing campaign effectiveness, and enhance user experience. You can choose to accept or refuse cookies, but refusing may impact website functionality.
